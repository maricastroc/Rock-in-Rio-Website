BEST CONCERTS OF ROCK IN RIO 2022
This selection was made according to the report of Globo News Portal's research

#1 Green Day

#1 Green Day

With 20 minutes, Green Day had already done by far the best concert of Rock in Rio: Billie Joe devilish, a fan on stage singing "Know your enemy", another couple of fans getting engaged and the biggest crowd they've ever managed to gather in front of "Palco Mundo" in 2022.

The repertoire was dominated by “American Idiot” (2004), with less space for the band's other great album, “Dookie” (1994).

#2 Coldplay

#2 Coldplay

The City of Rock has never been as bright and colorful as the Coldplay concert, which closed the "Palco Mundo" on Saturday. The English repeated two songs, “Viva la vida” and “Sky full of stars”.

Chris Martin praised the audience and said he wanted to sing the tracks with them again – without saying if the band was doing any recordings.

#3 Iron Maiden

#3 Iron Maiden

Despite the muffled sound, they put on a great concert for their fans. The group's concert, led by Bruce Dickinson, happened with a failure sound that hindered the beginning of the presentation.

The 64-year-old singer had to run to fix the damage, in order to win back the audience with a theatrical concert.

#4 Racionais MC's

#4 Racionais MC's

Racionais MC's played for the first time in their careers at a historic show at Rock in Rio. The group paid tribute to murdered black people, such as Marielle Franco and Agatha, by performing the song “Negro Drama”.

The stage had two large stairs at the back, as if it were a São Paulo city's train station.

#5 Justin Bieber

#5 Justin Bieber

A journey that began surrounded by expectations and with numerous rumors about cancellation ended in one of the most emblematic nights in the history of Rock in Rio.

Justin won US$5 million to perform at Rock in Rio. The artist was the most anticipated attraction of the edition since the festival and presented 23 hit songs.



WORST CONCERTS OF ROCK IN RIO 2022
This selection was made according to the report of Globo News Portal's research

#1 Avril Lavigne

#1 Avril Lavigne

The singer started Sunset's closing show with a huge sound failure. The worst thing is that Avril seemed lively and friendly, contrary to reports of a cold show in São Paulo.

But only those who were up front could understand and reciprocate. The finale even had its best ballad, "I'm with you", interrupted in the middle.

#2 Guns N' Roses

#2 Guns N' Roses

It wasn't a good day for Axl Rose - he even apologized for being "unwell". The vocalist hasn't been doing well for many years, but the combination of the torn voice with endless solos was a little more difficult for the audience on Thursday.

At the beginning of the encore, when they played "Patience", a good part of the audience had already lost their patience and holes appeared not far from the stage.

#3 Billy Idol

#3 Billy Idol

The most exciting moments in Billy Idol's show are still the same as they were over 30 years ago, when he was at Rock in Rio 1991. He even played some romantic ballads, such as "Eyes Without a Face".

But the live version was an embarrassment. Billy Idol got the entry wrong twice, it gets lost over time, and the arrangement has basically been simplified to compensate for the singer's limitations.

#4 Megan Thee Stallion

#4 Megan Thee Stallion

Megan Thee Stallion has enough talent for Palco Mundo and other big spaces in pop music. But the show's poor, convoluted production fell far short of her potential.

With no band, just a DJ and a maximum of 6 dancers on stage, she even seemed to have enough breath to get by. But the singer was so lost that she even made a call for fans for more than 15 minutes to take the stage, dance and line up to take selfies.

#5 Rita Ora

#5 Rita Ora

Rita Ora debuted at Rock in Rio in a warm-up for Dua Lipa, the night's main attraction. But the most sung song of the presentation was "Amor de Que", in the voice of the guest Pabllo Vittar.

It was as if Rita Ora was playing a TV presenter receiving a musical attraction on a Sunday show in the 90s.
